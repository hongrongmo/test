To sign up for a training session please go to http://elsevier.webex.com. Dates and times for sessions can be found by searching for Engineering Village 2. All times are listed in Eastern Standard Time (New York time).

These free training sessions will be given live via WebEx and hosted by the Ei librarian. For the training session you must have simultaneous access to an Internet connection and a voice phone line. Calls from within the United States and Canada are toll free. A confirmation letter will be sent with the Web address and phone number to call for the session.
