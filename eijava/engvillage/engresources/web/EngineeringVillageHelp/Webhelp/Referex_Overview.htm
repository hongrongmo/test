Referex Search Overview

Referex is the name of the eBooks collection on Engineering Village. Referex can be searched in two ways:

1. Search Referex from the Easy Search, Quick Search or Expert Search tab. Selecting any one of these tabs allows you to combine your Referex search with other EV databases such as Compendex, Inspec, etc., Alternatively, you can limit the search to Referex by checking off only the Referex checkbox.

2. Search Referex from the “eBook” tab: This option restricts your search to only eBooks. In addition, it gives you the ability to limit your search by Referex collection and to browse collections.

The example below illustrates a combined search from the Quick Search tab.

First, select the database. In this case, two databases have been selected: Compendex and Referex. Then, enter terms in the Search For field. You can limit your search by selecting a specific field using the pull-down menu to the right of the search box. For a combined Compendex & Referex search the pull down menu will present Keyword, Author, Title, and, Publisher. The specific pull down choices are dynamic and change according to the databases selected.

Keyword, the default search field, searches the text of the book collection and journal articles and returns pages of the book combined with records from Compendex.  Several key words can be combined using Boolean operators. Note: keyword searches can only be combined with other keyword searches. If one or more of the Search In boxes has keyword selected, author, ISBN, publisher, subject or title, cannot be searched. Non-keyword search terms can be combined.

 

eBook Search Tabs

Referex can also be searched using the eBook Search tab. Here you can select a specific collection or you could select all collections to search. Once the collection has been selected, you can then perform the search in the same manner as a Quick Search.

The example below illustrates the eBook Search screen.

Performing the search will generate a Search Results page, which lists all relevant book pages with full bibliographic information, including title, page number, chapter, author(s), ISBN, publisher and year published. Results can be sorted by relevance, author, source or publisher. You can further refine your results using the facets listed in the panel on the right of the screen (see Search Result section of Help File for more information).

From the Search Results page, you have the ability to view Page Details, Book Details or you could read the full text of the relevant page or chapter in PDF format.

Browse eBooks by Collection or Subject

To view a particular collection or subject, simply click its hyperlink under Browse Books by Collection or Subject. The search results will contain a complete list of titles by collection or subject, sorted by alphabetical order.
