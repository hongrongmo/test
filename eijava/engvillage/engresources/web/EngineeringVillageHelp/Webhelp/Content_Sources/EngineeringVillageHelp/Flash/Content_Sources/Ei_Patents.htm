Ei Patents

The patents databases on Engineering Village include US and European patents grants and applications. Ei Patents offers sophisticated search and retrieval tools across all 9.5 million patents registered with the US and European Patent Offices. US and European patent sources can be searched together or individually using Ei Patents on Engineering Village. The abstract records include patent classification codes and patent references and you can also find provided links to the full-text documents which can be acquired freely from the relevant patenting authority in addition to links from a patent to its citations within the published literature.
