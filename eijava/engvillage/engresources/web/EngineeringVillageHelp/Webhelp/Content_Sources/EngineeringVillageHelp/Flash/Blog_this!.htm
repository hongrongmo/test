What is a Blog?

A blog is a short form for web log; a personal journal published on the Web. This is a frequent, chronological publication of personal thoughts and Web links. More information on blogs can be found at Wikipedia. There are numerous blog hosts on the internet such as Blogger.com (www.blogger.com), Blogcity (www.blog-city.com), and others.

Click the "Blog This" button and select the content that is showing in the box and paste it in your post. Once you publish your post the title of the record will appear in your blog.
