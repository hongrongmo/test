Referex Search Results

Overview

Search results present citations matching your query. In Referex, a citation is a page from a book. These pages or citations are sorted by relevance by default. They can also be sorted by author, source and publisher. The search results view also displays facets vertically arranged in the right panel of the screen under the “Refine Results” heading. These facets can be used to refine your search.

 

There are four options for viewing specific citations in your search results:

1. Page Details
2. Book Details
3. Read Page
4. Read Chapter

For more information on these options, please see the “Viewing Your Referex Results” section of the Help File.

 

Refine Results

The eBook search results screen also allows you to quickly refine your search by using the facets on the right. A partial list of facets is displayed below.

 

Understanding the Facets (Refine Results)

Book Collection:  Lists the collections in which search results were found. The number in parentheses is the number of pages within the collection that contain the keyword.

Book Title: Lists the book titles in which the key words were found. The number in parentheses is the number of pages within a title that contain the keyword.

Book chapter: Lists all the chapters (book parts or chapters) within the books in which the keywords were found. The number in parentheses is the number of pages within a specific chapter that contain the keyword.

Keyword: Lists related keywords found in the relevant results. The number in parentheses is the number of pages within the result set that contain the key word.

Author: Lists the authors of the books retrieved.

Publisher: Lists the Publishers of the books retrieved.

Add a term: You can choose to refine your results by typing additional keywords in the "Add a term" box. You can than search for the keyword within your results or create a new search by choosing "Search all content".

Note: The number in brackets next to each term in the facets relates to the number of pages, not books.
