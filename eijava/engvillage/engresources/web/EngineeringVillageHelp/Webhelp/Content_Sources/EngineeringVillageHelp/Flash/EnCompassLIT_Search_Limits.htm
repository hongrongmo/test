EnCompassLIT Search Fields and Limits

All fields

Searching retrieves results from any of the following fields: Subject/Title/Abstract, Abstract, Author, Author affiliation, Title, Conference information, Publisher, Serial Title, Controlled Term and CAS Registry Number.

Subject/Title/Abstract

Searching retrieves results from any of the following fields: Subject, Abstract,  and Title, as well as Controlled term, and Uncontrolled terms. To search for an exact phrase, enclose term within braces or quotation marks:

{EMISSION CONTROL}

"DIESEL FUEL"

Search for chemical compounds with subscript or superscript (in fields other than controlled terms). Perform an exact match search using { }, as follows:

{AL sub 3} to search for Al3

{AL sup 3} to search for Al3

Do not use special characters such as <> ( ) in this syntax. Note, the application does not support concatenation of sub and sup.

Abstract

Search by single terms and/or phrases. To search for an exact phrase, enclose term within braces or quotation marks.

{FLUID CATALYTIC CRACKING}

"PETROLEUM DISTILLATION"

Author

Search in the format: LAST NAME - SPACE - FIRST INITIAL - SPACE - MIDDLE INITIAL. This field contains company names as well. Note that the number of initials may vary; also, on some names in older records, a suffix may be included, e.g., JR or III. TONG Y

When the initials are not known, use truncation (*):

MULLENIX*

When searching for authors, or an author, it is recommended that you use the Author Browse Index since author names may be cited in a variety of formats.

 

Author affiliation

Search by single words or phrases. To search for an exact phrase, enclose term within braces or quotation marks.

TOTALFINAELF

"LUBRIZOL CORP"

Prior to 2001, the official EnCompass policy was to provide the institutional affiliation of the first author or editor, if it could be determined from the source document. (Note: some author affiliation may appear in the author field.)

In 2001, this policy changed. The affiliation of the corresponding author is now given instead.

When searching for author affiliation it is recommended that you use the Author Affiliation Browse Index since author affiliation names may be cited in a variety of formats.

Title

Search by single words or phrases. To search for an exact phrase, enclose term within braces or quotation marks.

"EMISSON CONTROL"

CATALYST AND HYDROCONVERSION

Conference Information

Conference information includes the name, date, location and sponsor of a conference as well as the conference code number.  

Conference date is not searchable the way it is displayed.  For example, if the conference dates are May 6, 2007 through May 10, 2007, the search should be run for 20070506 AND 20070510.

Publisher

Search on the Publisher field to identify publishers, or find the journals published by a particular publisher. Be sure to look for all versions of a publisher's name. To do so, it is helpful to refer to the Publisher Browse Index.

Serial title

Search by single words or phrases. To search for an exact phrase, enclose term within braces or quotation marks. When searching for serial title it is recommended that you use the Serial Title Browse Index. Search by ISSN (International Standard Serial Number) for a specific journal. These terms also appear hyperlinked in the abstract and detailed record format. Clicking on any of these will retrieve records spanning the date range of the original search.

Controlled Term

Free-language retrieval is available with the EnCompassLIT but the most direct access to relevant items is through use of a controlled vocabulary. The controlled vocabulary used to index records in EnCompassLIT can be found in the EnCompass Thesaurus (The Authoritative Guide To Searching The EnCompass Technical Literature And Patent Databases). The latest edition of the EnCompass Thesaurus contains over 6,500 valid controlled terms. New controlled terms are added each year and are selected based on their usage in technical publications and recommendations of industry experts.

"THREE WAY CATALYST"

{ETHYLENE HOMOPOLYMER}

ANILINE

EnCompass controlled vocabulary is a list of subject terms used to describe the content of a document in the most specific and consistent way possible. These terms can be browsed in the Controlled term Browse Index.

For a more detailed explanation on how to search the EnCompassLIT, please refer to the EnCompassLIT/EnCompassPAT User Manual (A comprehensive manual for searches using the EnCompass technical literature (LIT) and patents (PAT and WPAM) databases).

These terms also appear hyperlinked in the abstract and detailed record format. Clicking on any of these will retrieve records spanning the date range of the original search.

CAS registry number

Search using registry numbers assigned by the Chemical Abstracts Service to chemical compounds. All valid Registry Numbers for EnCompassLIT are listed in Appendix C of the EnCompass Thesaurus.

Displayed CAS registry numbers (with the chemical name) are hyperlinked in the abstract and detailed record format. Clicking on any of these will retrieve records spanning the date range of the original search.

Limit by

Document type

Document types are used to describe the type of source publications from which indexed papers are taken. The document can be selected from a drop down menu. The document types used in EnCompassLIT are:

All document types (default)

Journal article - An independent section of text, usually with its own title and author statement, appearing in an issue of a journal, a journal being a periodical appearing at regular intervals (generally more frequently than annually) and intending to be continued indefinitely.

Conference - A conference paper issued as part of a proceedings whether published in a conference proceeding or in a journal.

Monograph Chapter – A chapter or section of a nonserial publication (i.e. a book), usually containing one volume, but possibly consisting of a finite number of volumes.

Report Chapter – A chapter or section of a report issued by

Report Review

Dissertation

Abstract - Abstracts from numerous other publications selected from secondary sources: Aqualine Abstracts, BMT Abstracts, Chemical Abstracts, Gas Abstracts Online (formerly Gas Abstracts), and Petroleum Abstracts (over 1200 titles).

Other - Dissertations, Reports…

Language

You can limit your search to any language listed in the drop-down menu: All languages, English, Chinese, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Russian or Spanish.

To search for more than one language, or to search for a language not listed in the Quick Search pull-down menu, use Expert Search.

Publication year(s)

Search by publication year of the record. Select the publication date in drop-down boxes, for example, 2000 to 2003

Select the same year in both date range drop-down boxes, for example, 2002 to 2002

Last four updates

Selecting this option allow you to limit your search to the four most recent weekly database updates.

Browse indexes

From the Browse Indexes box (located on the right hand side of the page); select the index you wish to use by clicking on the radio button next to it and then clicking on Browse.

Valid Browse Indexes for Quick Search in EnCompassLIT are:

Author

Author affiliation

Controlled term

Serial title

Publisher

Once the index is loaded, you can navigate by either selecting the first letter(s) of the term you wish to search for or by typing in the first few letters of the term in the SEARCH FOR box and clicking on Find.

In Quick Search, when you select a term from the index, it is automatically pasted into the first available textbox. The SEARCH FOR box will also be changed to the appropriate field. Un-selecting the term from the index will remove it from the textbox. If you select more than three terms, the fourth term will overwrite the term in the third textbox.

You can also choose to have the second and third index terms pasted from the indexes with either a Boolean AND or OR connecting them. See the Boolean Operators section of the Engineering Village Help File for more information on Boolean operators.
