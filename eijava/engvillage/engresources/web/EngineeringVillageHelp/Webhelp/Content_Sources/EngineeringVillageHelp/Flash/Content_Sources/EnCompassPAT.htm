EnCompassPAT

EnCompassPAT is the most authoritative patent database uniquely devoted to covering worldwide patents on the downstream petroleum, petrochemical, natural gas, energy and allied industries. All patents are specially selected by experts and indexed based on the EnCompass Thesaurus, facilitating precision searching via controlled vocabulary for accurate, relevant results.

EnCompassPAT database back to 1964 and contains over 450,000 records, and contains especially selected patents from 40 patenting authorities throughout the world, indexed on the basis of the EnCompass Thesaurus. More than 22,000 records are added annually, in weekly updates.

The following sections from the Derwent World Patents Index (DWPI) are the sources of patent abstracts:

Chemical Patents Index (since 1972):

Section A: (Polymers and Plastics)

Section D: (Food, Detergents, Water Treatment and Biotechnology)

Section E: (General Chemicals)

Section H: (Petroleum)

Section J: (Chemical Engineering)

Section M: (Metallurgy)

General & Mechanical Patents Index (since 1972, only US, GB & EP patents):

Section P: (General)

Section Q: (Mechanical)

The DWPI basic (alert) abstracts are used to make selections for electronic Patent Abstract bulletins and EnCompassPAT; the corresponding Derwent documentation abstracts, which contain more detailed information, are used for indexing.

From 1964-71, abstracts were written at EnCompass from copies of the original patents or from patent abstracts where available. From countries other than the US, abstracts were based on information in the official journal of the patent office, which sometimes was no more than the title of the patent plus names and dates .In the past, CAS abstracts were indexed for the countries for which Derwent did not write abstracts.
