Inspec

Inspec is a leading bibliographic database providing access to the world's scientific literature in electrical engineering, electronics, physics, control engineering, information technology, communications, computers, computing, and manufacturing and production engineering. The database contains over 11.2 million bibliographic records from scientific and technical journals and conference proceedings. Approximately 600,000 new records are added to the database annually.

Online coverage is from 1969 to the present, and records are updated weekly. Inspec is produced by the Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET).
