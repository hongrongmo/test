Ask an Expert

The “Ask an Expert” feature provides users with the opportunity to ask questions of either an EV Engineer, the Product Specialist, or the librarian of that user’s organization. The EV engineers are experts in chemical, mechanical, industrial, electrical, manufacturing, materials and computer-related engineering topics. All Ask an Expert services are complementary with the Engineering Village subscription.

Clicking the “Ask an Expert” tab on the www.engineeringvillage.com home page clearly displays three options of where to direct questions for a more user-friendly experience.  

For “Ask an Engineer” inquiries, the user can choose by subject area which engineering expert to email.  

Clicking on a particular speciality will open more options within that speciality. Clicking on the names of the experts will run a search for publications and/or patents that each expert is responsible for. Clicking the terms under each engineer's "area of expertise" will open search results for that term including controlled vocabulary and/or other fields.

In the “Ask a Product Specialist” section, users can ask EV’s Product Specialist about how to analyze results, learn more about using EV, or link to www.ei.org to view automated product tours and/or register for trainings. There is also a list of FAQs offering further assistance.

EV's Product Specialist provides regular online training sessions to customers and end users of Engineering Village. You must have simultaneous access to an Internet connection and a voice phone line to carry the audio. To sign up for a training session please click the "Register for a Training" button or contact eitraining@elsevier.com for additional details. Online seminars on Engineering Village are provided as a complementary service and are included in the library's subscription to the product.

Users who have questions about how to formulate expert searches, identify and locate a source for a book or an article, or find additional resources available at their institution or organization can contact their Librarian with one click using the “Ask a Librarian” Feature.   

Users can also link to www.ei.org to view automated product tours by clicking on the image above.

Please note: If a customer has chosen not have the “Ask a Librarian” feature turned on or we do not have current contact information for that librarian, “Ask a Librarian” questions from that organization will be sent to the EV Product Specialist.
