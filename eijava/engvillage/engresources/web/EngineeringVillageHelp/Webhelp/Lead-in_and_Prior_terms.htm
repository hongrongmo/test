Lead-in and Prior terms

In thesaurus pages, subject terms are hyperlinked; clicking on a term will bring you to its thesaurus record. Terms that appear in italics are "lead-in terms," which are either synonyms that are not part of the controlled vocabulary or prior terms that were formerly used for indexing and are still in the database in older records, but have been replaced by newer controlled terms. Any database search that involves the entire range of years should include both current and older controlled vocabulary. Prior terms are identified by an asterisk. Lead-in terms that are synonyms only, and that are not prior indexing terms, do not have a Select option.
