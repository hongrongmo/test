Using AiPs in Quick Searches

You can do the following with Articles in Press (AiPs) in a Quick Search:

  • Include AiPs with all other types of documents

  • Retrieve only AiPs in a Quick Search

  • Refine a Quick Search to exclude AiPs

  • Search for AiPs by publication year

To include AiPs with all other types of documents in a Quick Search

  1. On the Quick Search tab, click to check the Compendex database.

  2. Use the fields below the database to set up your search as desired.

  3. From the first drop-down in the Limit By section, accept the default of All document types.

  4. Select other limits or sort specifications as desired.

  5. Click Search.

  6. Scroll through results as needed to locate documents with the Article in Press designation.

To retrieve only AiPs in a Quick Search

  1. On the Quick Search tab, click to check the Compendex database.

  2. Use the fields below the database to set up your search as desired.

  3. From the first drop-down in the Limit By section, select Article in Press.

  4. Select other limits or sort specifications as desired.

  5. Click Search. Your search results contain only documents with the Article in Press designation.

Note: You can also display only AiPs by using the Refine Results pane as described in steps 7 and 8 in the next procedure; just click Include instead of Exclude.

Refine a Quick Search to exclude AiPs

  1. On the Quick Search tab, click to check the Compendex database.

  2. Enter the desired search terms.

  3. Use the fields below the database to set up your search as desired.

  4. From the first drop-down in the Limit By section, accept the default of All document types.

  5. Select other limits or sort specifications as desired.

  6. Click Search.

  7. To exclude AiPs, scroll down the Refine Results pane at right to Document type, and click to select Article in Press. (The number in parentheses reflects the number of AiPs included in your results.)

  8. Scroll back to the top of the Refine Results pane, and click Exclude. The system immediately removes AiPs from your search, and your number of results (directly below "Search Results") is reduced by the number of AiPs removed.

Search for AiPs by publication year

You might want to search for AiPs from a certain year.

  1. In any of the above search procedures—before clicking Search—use the date drop-downs to specify a beginning and ending year. For example, to limit your search to only AiPs published in 2010, select 2010 from both drop-downs.

  2. Follow the rest of your search procedure as described above, and click Search.

Your results contain only AiPs from the year specified.
