GEOBASE Special Index Fields

 Click here for a full list of all available search fields on Expert search

Regional descriptor

This data element is used for regional descriptors assigned to the record.

e.g. United States - Arizona - Grand Canyon

Atlantic Ocean - Mid - Atlantic Ridge

 

Species descriptor

Species are controlled against an authority list introduced in 1999. Taxonomic groups higher than genera (e.g. family names) are also indexed, though less commonly.

e.g. Oscillatoria bourellyi

Gerris buenoi
