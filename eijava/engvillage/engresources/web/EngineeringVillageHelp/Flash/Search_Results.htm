Search Results

 

Records retrieved from a patents search have the inventor/s name hyperlinked from the search results screen. When clicking the name a new search will be performed on the inventor's name, retrieving only patents that were granted to that person, whether alone or in collaboration with others.

 

To perform a search on the assignee to discover other patents issued to it, click on the detailed record view. The assignee name is hyperlinked within this view.
