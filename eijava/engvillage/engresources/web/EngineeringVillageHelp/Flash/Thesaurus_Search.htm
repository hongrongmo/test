Thesaurus Search

The thesauri are guides to the controlled vocabulary used in indexing articles for Compendex, Inspec, GEOBASE, ad GeoRef. Indexers choose terms from the controlled vocabulary to describe the article they are indexing. The controlled vocabulary is used to standardize the way the articles are indexed. The thesauri are hierarchical in nature. Terms are organized by broader, narrower or related concepts.

The GEOBASE database contains multiple types of controlled terms.  General controlled terms are stored in the Controlled Term field, which is searchable with the CV search code.  Regional controlled terms are stored in the Regional Term field, which is searchable with the RGI search code.  (More information about all search codes is available on the Expert Search screen.)

Because the GEOBASE thesaurus contains both general controlled terms and regional controlled terms, thesaurus queries on this database will search both these fields.  This search structured is slightly differently than the structure for Compendex, Inspec, and Georef queries which each search the Controlled Term field (CV) only.
