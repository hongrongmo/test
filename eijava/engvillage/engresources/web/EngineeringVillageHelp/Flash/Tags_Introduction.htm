Introduction to Tags & Groups

Tagging is a user-defined taxonomy. Such taxonomy is sometimes called a folksonomy and the bookmarks are referred to as tags.

For more information about tagging, its benefits and usages please see: www.en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Folksonomy

With Engineering Village tagging you can add, edit ad share tags you assigned to records.

You can create groups by inviting people with the same research interest as you to join a group with which you can share tagged records and view records shared by the group.

See viewing tags + groups for more information

Note: Tagging records on Engineering Village requires having a personal account
