Cited by Count from Scopus

The Cited by count appears in search results near each Compendex and Inspec article that contains one or more citations within Scopus. Following is an example of a Cited by count noted in search results:

The number of documents that cite the article appears in parentheses, as shown in the example. If an article has no citations, the Cited by feature does not appear.

When you click the Cited by link, a Scopus window opens where you can view a list of articles that cite the current article:

All EV users will have access to Scopus, regardless of their Scopus subscription status. Non-subscribers of Scopus can view all articles on the Scopus Preview page with limited functionality. You can work further with your data in Scopus according to your subscription status.

Viewing the Article's Abstract

When you click the Abstract link on the Search Results page, the abstract appears with the Tools in Scopus box to the right of the results, as shown below:

Under Tools in Scopus, clicking the number of times cited (in the example above, 21 times) opens the same Scopus window as shown previously.

Other Views Possible

  • Detailed - Click Detailed (beside the Abstract link) to see the abstract plus an easy-to-read list of details, including the abbreviated source title, language the article is written in, and the number of references in the article.

  • Article title under Tools in Scopus - Under Tools in Scopus, click the hyperlinked title of the article to open a Scopus view similar to the one shown above.

  • Learn more about Scopus - Under Tools in Scopus, click this link to gain a better understanding of the Scopus application.
