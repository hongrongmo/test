accession number

The accession number is a unique number assigned to each record in Compendex. It  is only displayed in the detailed record format. In the detailed display, an Article in Press record displays a temporary accession number in alphanumeric format, such as IP12345678. When the Article in Press is replaced in the future with the final version of the record, it will display a permanent Compendex accession number, such as 20100123456789.

Article in Press (AIP)

A document in the Compendex database that has been accepted for publication but has not yet, in most cases, been assigned to a journal issue. It is an article that is still in progress.  See also accession number.
