Cited by Link

The Cited by link appears in each EV Compendex article that contains one or more citations in the List, Abstract, and Detailed formats. Following is an example:

The number of documents that cite the article appears in parentheses, as shown in the example. If an article has no citations, the Cited by feature does not appear. However, it does appear for all EV users, regardless of their Scopus subscription status.

When you click the Cited by link, a window appears where you can view all articles that cite the current article. Non-subscribers of Scopus can view all articles on the Scopus Preview page with limited functionality.
