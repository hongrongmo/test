Tagging a Record

From a record view you can assign one or more tags to a record.

You can decide to make a tag publicly viewable, keep it private or limited its view to your institution. When you create a group, you will also have an option to share the tags you assigned with a group. See Creating Groups for more details.

Tagging on Engineering Village can be shared and viewed by customers only. If you would like to make the tags available on public tagging services such as the 2collab or del.icio.us, click on the 2collab or del.icio.us link at the bottom the Tag this page/abstract box.

 

 

There are three main types of tags that can be assigned to records:

Public – these tags can be viewed by all Engineering Village customers.

My Institution – these tags can be viewed by all uses logged in from your institution.

Private – these tags will be viewed only by you.

Note: When groups are created, you can choose to assign tags to groups. In the example below a group titled "carbon nanotubes" was created.

Select your own key words (tags) to be assigned to a record in the space provided and click “add”. Separate each term with a comma.

When you type a word, a drop down menu will open up containing terms from our free language vocabulary. You may choose to use one of these terms or assign your own term/s to the record. If you would like to tag a phrase such as carbon nanotubes do not use quotations, rather separate between the single terms and the phrase by either a space or a comma as shown below.
