EnCompassLIT

EnCompassLIT™ is a bibliographic database specifically devoted to covering technical literature published worldwide on the downstream petroleum, petrochemical, natural gas, energy and allied industries. Upstream coverage focuses solely on oil field chemicals. The EnCompass Thesaurus facilitates precision searching via controlled vocabulary, meaning that all records in EnCompassLIT™ are specially indexed for accurate, relevant results.

Dating back to 1964, EnCompassLIT covers over 1500 scientific journals, trade magazines, conference papers and technical reports from 20 countries, in 10 languages. The EnCompass Source Guide has a section listing Publications by Category, Country of Publication Index, as well as information about the year EnCompass coverage began, title changes, and scope of coverage for each publication. The database contains over 860,000 records and more than 25,000 records are added annually. EnCompassLIT is updated weekly.
