GEOBASE

GEOBASE is a multidisciplinary database of bibliographic information and abstracts that deeply indexes research literature across the earth sciences including geology, human and physical geography, environmental sciences, oceanography and geomechanics. Spanning over 25 years (1980-present) of research literature, GEOBASE addresses increasingly important environmental issues such as alternative energy sources, pollution, waste management and nature conservation. Covering over 2,000 titles including peer-reviewed journals, trade publications, book series and conference proceedings, GEOBASE has the most international coverage of any database in the field, covering publications from over 60 countries.  Updated weekly, GEOBASE contains 1.8 million records with over 100,000 new records added annually.
