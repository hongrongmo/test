Using AiPs in Expert Searches

You can do the following with Articles in Press (AiPs) in an Expert Search:

  • Include AiPs with all other types of documents

  • Retrieve only AiPs in an Expert Search

  • Refine an Expert Search to exclude AiPs

  • Search for AiPs by publication year

To include AiPs with all other types of documents in an Expert Search

    1. On the Expert Search tab, click to check the Compendex database.

    2. Enter the search terms in the box.

    3. Use the Search From and Sort By options to set up your search as desired.

    4. Click Search.

    5. Scroll through results as needed to locate documents with the Article in Press designation.

To retrieve only AiPs in an Expert Search

    1. On the Expert Search tab, click to check the Compendex database.

    2. Enter the desired search term(s).

    3. In the Browse Indexes box, click Document type.

    4. In the pop-up box, click the AND radio button, and click to check IP - Article in Press.

    5. Close the pop-up. The search terms box is now populated.

    6. Use the Search From and Sort By options to further set up your search as desired.

    7. Click Search. All of your results have the Article in Press designation.

Note: You can also display only AiPs by using the Refine Results pane as described in steps 5 and 6 in the next procedure; just click Include instead of Exclude.

Refine an Expert Search to exclude AiPs

    1. On the Expert Search tab, click to check the Compendex database.

    2. Enter the search terms in the box.

    3. Use the Search From and Sort By options to set up your search as desired.

    4. Click Search. Your results appear.

    5. To exclude AiPs, scroll down the Refine Results pane at right to Document type, and click to check Article in Press. (The number in parentheses reflects the number of AiPs included in your results.)

    6. Scroll to the top or bottom of the Refine Results pane, and click Exclude. The system immediately removes AiPs from your search, and your number of results (directly below "Search Results") is reduced by the number of AiPs removed.

Search for AiPs by publication year

You might want to search for AiPs from a certain year.

  1. In any of the above search procedures—before clicking Search—use the date drop-downs to specify a beginning and ending year. For example, to limit your search to only AiPs published in 2010, select 2010 from both drop-downs.

  2. Follow the rest of your search procedure as described above, and click Search.

Your results contain only AiPs from the year specified.
